Our Story

A Notary You Can Trust

NotaryPro was founded with a simple mission: make professional notary services accessible, convenient, and affordable for everyone. After over a decade serving the greater Los Angeles area, we've built a reputation on professionalism, accuracy, and reliability.

We understand that your time is valuable. That's why we come to you — at your home, office, hospital bedside, or any location that works best. Whether it's a loan signing at midnight or emergency power of attorney at a hospital, we're there when it matters most.

Our Process

How We Work

A streamlined, professional process to ensure your documents are handled correctly from start to finish.

Step 1

Initial Contact

Call, text, or book online. We confirm your document type, availability, and location preference.

Step 2

Appointment Confirmation

We confirm the time, location, and document requirements. You receive a confirmation message.

Step 3

Document Review

On arrival, documents are reviewed for completeness and the signers are identified per legal requirements.

Step 4

Notarization

Documents are signed, sealed, and notarized correctly. Every detail is checked for accuracy.

Step 5

Completion & Delivery

You receive your notarized documents and a receipt. For loan signings, packages are returned to title companies promptly.
